Resident Evil 4 Remake (2023)

An in-depth look at the remake of a classic survival horror game.

About the Game

Resident Evil 4 Remake (RE4R) is a reimagining of the original 2005 game, considered a landmark title in the survival horror genre. Developed by Capcom, this remake aims to capture the essence of the original while offering modernized graphics and gameplay.

This iteration of Resident Evil 4 introduces a plethora of improvements, including revamped visuals with enhanced details, updated gameplay mechanics for smoother controls, and a more immersive experience overall.

RE4R also features expanded storylines, delving deeper into the lore of the Resident Evil universe. Players can expect new narrative elements that complement the familiar storyline, providing both newcomers and longtime fans with fresh perspectives and surprises.

Additionally, Capcom has incorporated exclusive in-game rewards for pre-orders, enticing players with bonus content and incentives to secure their copy early. The game also offers integration with online multiplayer modes, enhancing the replay value and fostering a vibrant community.

Storyline

Set in rural Europe, Resident Evil 4 Remake follows the journey of Leon S. Kennedy, a former Raccoon City police officer, as he embarks on a perilous mission to rescue the President's daughter, Ashley Graham, from a mysterious cult known as Los Illuminados.

As Leon navigates through the eerie village of Pueblo and encounters hordes of infected villagers controlled by Los Illuminados, he gradually uncovers a dark conspiracy that threatens not only Ashley's life but also global security.

Editions

Resident Evil 4 Remake is available in several editions, each tailored to meet the preferences of different players:

  • Standard Edition: Includes the base game.
  • Deluxe Edition: Offers additional digital content, such as exclusive costumes, weapons, and in-game items. (Deluxe Edition has been discontinued since the release of the Gold Edition)
  • Gold Edition (New): The most comprehensive edition, featuring all content from the Deluxe Edition along with exclusive physical collectibles, artbooks, and soundtrack CDs.

Characters

Resident Evil 4 Remake features a diverse cast of characters, each with their own motivations and roles in the unfolding narrative:

  • Leon S. Kennedy: The protagonist and seasoned survivor, determined to rescue Ashley at any cost.
  • Ashley Graham: The President's daughter, kidnapped by Los Illuminados for sinister purposes.
  • Ada Wong: A mysterious figure with her own agenda, whose alliances remain ambiguous.
  • Bitores Mendez: The imposing chief of the rural village and a key player in Los Illuminados' schemes.
  • Ramon Salazar: A cunning and eccentric nobleman allied with the cult, wielding significant influence.

Gameplay Mechanics

Resident Evil 4 Remake introduces a blend of classic and modern gameplay mechanics. The over-the-shoulder perspective remains, offering a familiar yet enhanced combat experience. Key features include:

  • Overhauled aiming and shooting mechanics for more precise control.
  • Expanded inventory management system with the iconic Attaché Case.
  • New stealth mechanics allowing for silent takedowns and strategic planning.
  • Enhanced melee combat with new moves and counterattacks.
  • Environmental interactions, such as setting traps and utilizing cover.
